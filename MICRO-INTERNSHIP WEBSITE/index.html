About Micro Internships

Micro internships are short-term, project-based internships that give students the opportunity to work on real-world problems and gain valuable experience. They typically last from a few days to a few weeks.

Benefits for Students

  • Gain practical experience in a short period of time.
  • Build your resume with relevant projects.
  • Network with professionals in your field.
  • Explore potential career paths and industries.

How to Apply

Applying for micro internships is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Search for available micro internships on job boards and company websites.
  2. Prepare a resume and cover letter tailored to the specific internship.
  3. Submit your application and follow up as needed.
  4. Prepare for an interview by researching the company and practicing common questions.
